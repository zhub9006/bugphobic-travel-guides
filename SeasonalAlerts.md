# 📅 Seasonal Bug Alerts by World Region

Stay informed about when bugs are most active worldwide to plan the safest trips!

## Africa
- Sub-Saharan Africa: Year-round (peak rainy) — Malaria mosquitoes — Dry season
- North Africa (Sahara): Minimal — Almost bug-free — Year-round
- Madagascar: Oct–Mar (malaria) — Malaria, Rift Valley fever — May–Sep

## North America
- Southern US (Texas, Florida): Mar–Nov — Mosquitoes, fire ants — Nov–Feb
- Pacific Northwest (Portland, OR): Rare — Occasional gnats — Jun–Sep
- Southwest US (Sedona, AZ): Negligible — Near-zero year-round — Year-round!
- Hawaii: Mar–Nov (windward) — Mosquitoes on wet side — Apr–Oct (Kona)
- Canada (Maritimes): Jun–Aug — Black flies, mosquitoes — Sep–Oct

## Central America / Caribbean
- Caribbean most islands: Jun–Nov — Mosquitoes, sandflies — Dec–May
- Costa Rica: Year-round (rainy worse) — Mosquitoes — Dec–Apr
- Montserrat: May–Nov — Volcanic areas safer — Jan–Apr
- Curaçao: Sep–Dec — Sandflies possible — Jan–Aug

## South America
- Atacama Desert, Chile: Negligible — Near-zero year-round — Year-round!
- Patagonia: Dec–Feb (valleys) — Few flies, no mosquitoes — Nov, Mar–Apr
- Amazon Basin: Year-round (peak rainy) — Malaria, dengue — Avoid!
- Andes Mountains: Minimal — Fewer insects — Dry season

## Europe
- Iceland: Zero year-round — Nothing! — Year-round
- Norway (mainland fjords): Jun–Aug — Biting midges — Sep–May
- Svalbard: Zero year-round — Nothing! — Jun–Aug
- Scotland: Mid-May–Sept — Midges! — Oct–Apr
- London, England: Minimal — Occasional midges — Apr–Jun
- Mediterranean: Jun–Sept — Mosquitoes near still water — Apr–May, Oct

## Asia
- Gobi Desert, Mongolia: May–Jul (riverbanks) — Mosquitoes, ticks — Sep–Oct
- Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia: Jun–Aug (dusk only) — Occasional mosquitoes — Sep–Oct
- The Maldives: May–Oct (local islands) — Mosquitoes on local islands — Nov–Apr
- Southeast Asia: Year-round (peak rainy) — Malaria, dengue, Zika — Avoid!
- Southern Japan: Jun–Sept — Mosquitoes after rain — Oct–May

## Oceania
- New Caledonia: Nov–Apr (wet) — Sandflies, cyclone risk — May–Oct
- French Polynesia: Nov–Apr — Mosquitoes — May–Oct
- Australia (Tropical North): Oct–May — Malaria mosquitoes — Apr–Sep
- Australia (South/Tasmania): Summer — Moderate mosquitoes — Sep–Nov, Mar–May

## High-Risk Regions to AVOID (Bug-Phobic Travelers)
- Amazon Basin — Malaria, dengue year-round → Atacama, Patagonia
- Sub-Saharan Africa — Malaria, diseases → North Africa, Iceland
- Southeast Asia — Malaria, dengue → Mongolia, Gobi
- Central America — High mosquitoes → Montserrat, Curaçao
- Indonesian tropics — Malaria, dengue → Maldives resorts
