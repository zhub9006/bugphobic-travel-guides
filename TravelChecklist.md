# 🧳 Universal Bug-Phobic Travel Checklist

Always packed, no matter where you travel:

## Essential Gear
- **Permethrin-treated clothing** — kills insects on contact
- **DEET or Picaridin repellent (20%+)** — proven deterrent
- **Mosquito net** — for any tropical accommodation
- **Bandana / head net** — face and neck protection at dusk
- **Repellent wristbands** — added DEET layer

## Hotel/Room Strategy
- **Request a high floor** — bugs travel upward, not down
- **Use the AC/fan on high** — mosquitoes can't fly in strong air currents
- **Keep doors and windows closed** — prevents entry at dusk
- **Pack a travel fan** — portable breeze = bug-free zone
- **Check bedding on arrival** — inspect for bed bugs
- **Seal food in containers** — prevents attracting ants

## Behavioral Tips
- **Stay indoors at dusk/dawn** — peak mosquito biting time
- **Wear light colors** — dark colors attract mosquitoes
- **Avoid perfumes** — scented products attract insects
- **Choose windy/exposed areas** — strong wind = fewer bugs
- **Pick desert/mountain destinations** — no water = no bugs
- **Travel in cool/dry seasons** — insects need warmth and moisture

## Destination Selection
| If you want... | Go to... |
|---------------|----------|
| Absolute zero bugs | Antarctica, Iceland, Svalbard |
| Tropical vibes, no bugs | resort Maldives, Curaçao, Montserrat |
| Desert adventure, no bugs | Atacama, Gobi, Sedona |
| Cool climate, no bugs | London, Portland, Patagonia |
